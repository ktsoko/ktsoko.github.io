Locksport Events

Team building, happy hours, conferences — we do it all. Let’s spice up your next event with a mind-opening experience.

What is Locksport?

Locksport is the ethical discovery and execution of faults within lock systems. We follow two strict rules: don’t pick a lock that isn’t yours and don’t pick a lock in use. These rules help you avoid legal trouble, reduce the potential of damage to your locks, and prevent you from having to call a locksmith in case of a failure in the lock. We take these rules very seriously.

“Now that I know how to pick I’ll never be locked out again!” We highly suggest against this mentality. If you forget your keys a lot, invest in a key hider (or develop a trusting relationship with a neighbor).

It is always a good idea to get to know your local locksmith. Locksmiths can help you when you’re locked out, but can also advise on your current security system (locks and beyond), install or repair locks and hardware, and it’s comforting to have a previous relationship before you’re in a jam. Find your local brick-and-mortar locksmith and tell them District Locksport sent you!

What is a Locksport Event?

Locksport events come in many flavors and can be suited to your venue and audience. A typical event consists of a brief walkthrough of locks and picking principles, hands-on mentoring, and more fun picking-adjacent activities (restraint escape, races and games, giveaways).

A happy hour with snacks and drinks is always a good format — people are more patient with their blood sugar up, and alcohol can help relax attitudes and fingers. Zen and relaxation, whether natural or chemically-induced, play a big part in lock picking.

Lunch-and-learns, a table at events, brunches, and even formal conference room events area have all been successful. I can’t remember anyone leaving one of my events angry — antsy to get their hands on the next lock, yes, but never angry.
