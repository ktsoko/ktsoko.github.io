What is Locksport?

There are hidden systems we interact with daily without giving any thought to how they work. Locksport is about exploring one of the most ubiquitous black box systems—locks! Discover the inner workings of an integral part of your life. Find out how locks work and how their construction allows their defeat. Apply the same mindfulness to your everyday interactions and you’ll find patterns and puzzles you had never considered before. Learn about events.
